About Furni

We are passionate about creating beautiful, functional spaces that inspire and comfort. Our journey began with a simple belief: that everyone deserves to live in a home that reflects their personality and meets their needs.

Since our founding in 2010, we've been dedicated to bringing you the finest furniture and home decor from around the world. Our carefully curated collection combines timeless design with modern functionality.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leaders

A Journey of Passion and Innovation

What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted names in furniture and home decor. Our founder, Sarah Johnson, began with a simple vision: to make beautiful, quality furniture accessible to everyone.

Today, we continue to honor that vision by sourcing the finest materials, working with skilled artisans, and maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service.
